Privacy Policy - Gmail Reader

Effective Date: October 18, 2025

1. Information We Collect

Gmail Reader accesses your Gmail data only for the purpose of email processing automation. Specifically:

  • Email content (subject, body, sender, recipients)
  • Email metadata (date, labels, read status)
  • Gmail account information (email address for identification)

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Process emails according to your automation rules
  • Extract data for integration with workflow tools
  • Organize emails with labels and read/unread status
  • Provide automated email processing services

3. Data Storage and Security

  • We DO NOT permanently store your email content
  • Only temporary processing data is held in memory during operation
  • All connections use HTTPS/TLS encryption
  • OAuth tokens are stored securely on your designated server

4. Data Sharing

  • We DO NOT sell, trade, or share your Gmail data with third parties
  • Data is only used for your authorized automation purposes
  • No advertising or marketing use of your data

5. Your Rights

  • Revoke access at any time through Google Account settings
  • Request deletion of any stored authentication tokens
  • Modify or discontinue service usage

6. Google API Services User Data Policy Compliance

Gmail Reader's use and transfer of information received from Google APIs adheres to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.

7. Data Retention

  • Email content is processed in real-time and not stored
  • Only OAuth authentication tokens are retained for service functionality
  • Tokens are automatically refreshed according to Google's policies
  • All data is deleted upon service discontinuation

8. Third-Party Services

Gmail Reader integrates with:

  • Google Gmail API: For email access (governed by Google's privacy policy)
  • Automation Platforms: For workflow integration (n8n, etc.)

Each integration is limited to the specific data you authorize.

9. Contact Information

For privacy concerns or questions:

  • Website: https://donam.vn
  • Address: DoNam Technology Solutions, Vietnam

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify users of any material changes by updating the "Effective Date" at the top of this policy.
